Don't call it a throwback. Twisty Tyrant may be Coaster Coast's oldest and most classic coaster.

This coaster still competes with some of the park's heavy hitters. It's one for the ages and has been a family favorite since it opened in 1964. Some roller coaster aficionados may even call it a bucket list coaster. Twisty Tyrant is packed with hills, thrills and history.

Height Requiremnet: 48" minimum.

A looming giant amongst a park full of them.

Night Train was designed for the purpose of proving bigger is better. In fact, Night Train's creation demanded an all-new category just to classify its one of a kind nature - thus was born the giga-coaster. Ranking as one of the world's best steel coasters, this behemoth shoots riders over hills, past lagoons and through tunnels, all at unthinkable speeds.
